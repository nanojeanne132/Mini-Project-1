Fundraising events

Zumba for a cause

05/20/2020

Philippine Breast Cancer Foundation Organization have recently planning in organizing an event which is Zumba for a cause that will be held on March 20, 2020. The event will have a possible participants that will range from 200 or more people. The target participants are those who are at the age of 18 years old up to 50 years old and the event will start between 6 to 7 in the morning. The venue of the event will be in Melvin Jones. The event was develop fitness and improve the stamina as well as instill unity among the participants that has a different ages. Moreover, the Zumba for a cause event could be a way not just to develop their healthy body conditions but they will be able to help the people who are in need. The funds that accumulated through this event will be donated to a chosen charity. The participant will benefit from this event by having a healthy lifestyle and be aware that there are other better way of exercising or being fit and that is Zumba. This event is not just a Zumba, The main purpose of this event is to raise fund to be able to donate in a charity organization. This Zumba for a cause will serve as a genuine contribution for those people that should be given an attention. Specially those orphan children, special or disabled person, and old peoplewho has been abandon by their own family or relatives.

Fun run for a cause

04/20/2020

We organized a fun run for those who are willing to join the movement against breast cancer. This way, we are able to garner much attention to give this event a successful event. Numerous participants from other places and ethnicities came to gather and distinct in the Camp John Hay as our venue for the run, they were classified according to there preferences in the category namely: the 3k run for teens; 5k run for amateurs; and 15k run for those who are athletes. Each having also their own respective prizes, tokens, and certificate for those who completed the said run.

Concert for a cause

05/20/2022

This concert for a cause will become a part of one of our fundraising programs this year! The said event will become one of the biggest event in the City of Baguio that held on the year 2022. As it was announce that there are some invited known to be iconic bands not just in the locally but also international bands to be able to perform on the big stage at Melvin Jones. Some of these iconic performers are namely: Maroon 5, Ben and Ben, Lady Gaga, and many other more. The event is already successful since the first day the tickets were launched, as the tickets were already sold out the next day. By the time, during the event, it had garnered hundred thousand viewers to visit the place and had also raised awareness to them. On the reason to say that the event made a huge impact to organization and to fundraising activity of PCBF. Thanks to the staffs and the organizers they also performed well, as the bands and viewers were satisfied on how they manage the performers in the stage and to the people who bought the ticket. The organization were able to raise funds and to sustain the organization. The concert for a cause is truly indeed an event of the year they said. All of had happened during the event was fantastic as it was able to raise fund of up to 100,000,000 Pesos for it's funds. It became a gateway for hope and luckily it made way to help to those who are a in need of help, It is one of a kind concert of the century.
